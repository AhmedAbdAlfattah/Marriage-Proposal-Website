Are Getting Married

August 26th, 2017

He Proposed

On a sunny day in Laguna Beach, California.

Mustafa made reservations at Las Brisas and as far I knew we were just headed to a nice dinner. We arrived there a little early so he suggested we take a walk down to the beach. After several steps, I noticed there were two photographers there taking pictures of the scenery. I just kept wondering why we seemed to be in a lot of their shots. As we walked on I noticed some writing in the sand below, but at that point it was still too far to read.

Once we got closer, I recognized my name. I could now see that the writing in the sand said “Marry me Jaspreet.” I became teary-eyed as I realized Mustafa set this whole thing up, and he hired the photographers to take pictures of us.

Mustafa got down on one knee, and pulled out the engagement ring. Of course I said Yes!!!
